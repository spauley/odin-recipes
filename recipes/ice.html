Ice

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes. I find it pairs well with a number of drinks such as water, soda, and juice, and is an essential ingredient in iced tea.

Making this recipe always brings back memories of my childhood, where my mother would often prepare ice for its health benefits -- just apply it to a swollen area!

Warning: this dish should not be consumed directly, as it could lead to tooth breakage. Rather, serve it after it has been processed in a blender, or combine with liquid and drink the liquid around it.

Ingredients and Equipment

  • water
  • ice cube tray
  • freezer

Steps

  1. Fill ice cube tray with water.
  2. Carefully move ice cube tray to a level surface in the freezer.
  3. Wait a minimum of 6 hours. Opening freezer to check on progress may result in a longer preparation time.
  4. Enjoy!
