Toast

Description

Toast is a quick and simple breakfast. It can be combined with lots of other foods to create a more complex meal, or stand on its own.

Ingredients and Equipment

  • Bread
  • Toaster

Steps

  1. Put a slice of bread into the toaster.
  2. Choose a medium heat setting, and depress the button to start the toaster.
  3. Wait for the toaster to pop up the bread, indicating it has finished.
  4. Enjoy!
