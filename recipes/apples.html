Apples

Description

This recipe is for apples. Apples can be red, yellow, or green. They can also be brown. You should not eat them if they are brown.

You may know apples from the following adage: "An apple a day keeps the doctor away." This is not a scientifically proven statement.

Ingredients and Equipment

  • Apples
  • Knife (optional)

Steps

  1. Drive to the store.
  2. Find the produce section.
  3. Buy some apples.
  4. Drive home and rinse apples in the sink.
  5. (Optional) Cut apples with the knife into smaller pieces.
  6. Eat the apple, avoiding the inner core!
